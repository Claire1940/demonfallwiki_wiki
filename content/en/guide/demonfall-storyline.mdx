<Section icon={Target} title="The Slayer Exam and Final Selection">

To progress through the **demonfall storyline**, every aspiring Slayer must pass the Final Selection. This grueling exam takes place in a demon-infested forest where survival is the only goal. You are required to eliminate a specific number of demons to prove your worth to the Corps.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Urokodaki">
    Find Akira Urokodaki to initiate the quest for the Final Selection. He will provide the necessary guidance and the iconic mask required for the exam.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Forest">
    Travel to the exam site. Once inside, you cannot leave until the objective is met or you are defeated.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Demon Cull">
    You must defeat 30 demons in total. This includes standard demons and the more dangerous Blue Demon, which possesses higher health and stronger attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Completion">
    After securing 30 kills, return to the exit to receive your Demon Slayer uniform and your first official rank within the organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Demon Type | Difficulty | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Demon** | Low | Basic M1 combos and heavy attacks |
| **Blue Demon** | Medium | Use breathing techniques to break guard |
| **Group Mobs** | High | AoE skills like Clear Blue Sky are essential |

<Callout type="warning" title="Exam Requirements">
Do not attempt to exit the forest before reaching the 30-kill threshold. The game tracks your progress, and leaving early will result in a failed attempt, forcing you to restart the exam.
</Callout>

</Section>

The fight against Akuro serves as your introduction to boss mechanics, requiring you to dodge telegraphed attacks and exploit openings. Rui, however, introduces long-range thread attacks that can trap and drain your health quickly. The Lower Moon 2 encounter is currently the pinnacle of the storyline progression, testing everything you have learned about your chosen Breathing Style.

| Boss Name | Health Pool | Notable Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Akuro** | ~1,500 HP | EXP, Yen, Slayer Rank Up |
| **Rui** | ~2,500 HP | Massive EXP, Rare Materials |
| **Lower Moon 2** | ~4,000 HP | Top-tier EXP, Story Completion |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Efficiency">
Using Sun Breathing techniques like "Solar Heat Haze" or "Clear Blue Sky" provides the best damage-to-stamina ratio for boss fights. Always maintain at least 20% stamina to ensure you can dash away from ultimate attacks.
</Callout>
