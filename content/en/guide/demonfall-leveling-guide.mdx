<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Kamakura Raid Game Mode">

For players looking to maximize their experience gains during the late game, the Kamakura Raid is the premier method. This game mode is designed specifically for high-level progression and offers the largest single payout of experience in the game. However, players must meet strict prerequisites to participate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Prestige 5">
    Unlock the ability to host and participate in the raid by reaching Prestige level 5.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to Kamakura Village">
    Locate the travel NPC in the main village and pay the required fee to teleport to Kamakura Village.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Form a Raid Party">
    Assemble a team of players to handle the scaling difficulty of the incoming enemy waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete 20 Waves">
    Defeat all 20 waves of enemies to secure the maximum reward of 189,000 XP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Completing all 20 waves of the Kamakura Raid rewards players with 189,000 XP, making it the fastest way to reach maximum prestige. Because of the high XP payout, the raid is balanced with a strict cooldown timer. Once a raid is completed or failed, players must wait before they can initiate another run.

| Wave Milestone | XP Earned | Cooldown | Recommended Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wave 10** | 45,000 XP | 2 Hours | Level 40+ |
| **Wave 20 (Clear)** | 189,000 XP | 2 Hours | Level 50 |

During the raid, prioritize crowd control and area-of-effect breathing styles to clear waves quickly. Defending the objective is critical, as losing too many players early in the run will result in failure and trigger the cooldown timer, wasting potential XP gains.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
The Kamakura Raid has a strict 2-hour cooldown. Ensure your party is fully prepared and equipped with optimal builds before starting to avoid wasting the run.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Infinity Castle Boss Rush">

The Infinity Castle is a specialized instance that offers a structured boss rush experience. This mode is highly recommended for mid-to-late game players who want to supplement their leveling routine, especially during the cooldown periods of the Kamakura Raid.

<Checklist
  id="infinity-castle-prep"
  title="Infinity Castle Unlock Checklist:"
  items={[
    "Complete the Lower Moon 2 mission to unlock the castle",
    "Equip a high-damage Breathing Style or Blood Demon Art",
    "Stock up on recovery items before entering the instance",
    "Form a party to handle the consecutive boss fights"
  ]}
/>

Inside the Infinity Castle, players face three powerful bosses sequentially: Akaza, Doma, and Kokushibo. Each boss defeated rewards a significant amount of experience, totaling 21,000 XP for a full completion. The structured nature of the run makes it a reliable source of progression.

| Boss Name | XP Granted | Key Mechanic | Threat Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Akaza** | 6,000 XP | Rapid physical strikes | Medium |
| **Doma** | 7,000 XP | Ice area-of-effect attacks | High |
| **Kokushibo** | 8,000 XP | Long-range crescent slashes | Very High |

Akaza focuses on close-quarters combat and can be countered with well-timed parries. Doma creates freezing zones that slow and damage players, requiring constant movement. Kokushibo is the final and most dangerous threat, utilizing wide-reaching slashes that can deplete a health bar instantly if not dodged.

<Callout type="info" title="Key Info">
Completing the Lower Moon 2 mission is a permanent unlock for the Infinity Castle. Once unlocked, you can run this instance repeatedly to bridge the gap between raid cooldowns.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Demonfall Leveling Guide: Optimal Progression Paths">

To level up efficiently, players should structure their progression based on their current Prestige level and game stage. Different methods become viable as your character grows stronger and gains access to new areas.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Early Game (Prestige 0-4)">
    Focus entirely on **boss farming**. Target Kaigaku and Gyutaro in groups to gain 6,000 XP per kill without the need for high-end gear.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mid Game (Prestige 5)">
    Unlock the **Kamakura Raid**. Prioritize completing the 20 waves for 189,000 XP whenever the 2-hour cooldown is active.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Late Game (Prestige 5+)">
    Alternate between **Infinity Castle runs** and Kamakura Raids. Use the 21,000 XP from the castle to fill the raid cooldown periods.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The transition between these stages depends heavily on your character build. Players utilizing support-oriented styles should focus on group content, while high-damage solo builds can attempt boss farming and Infinity Castle runs independently.

| Progression Stage | Primary Method | Secondary Method | Est. XP per Hour |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Game** | Kaigaku Farming | Gyutaro Farming | 36,000 XP |
| **Mid Game** | Kamakura Raid | Boss Farming | 75,000 XP |
| **Late Game** | Kamakura Raid | Infinity Castle | 115,000 XP |

Maximizing your XP per hour requires minimizing downtime. Always coordinate with a consistent party to ensure rapid clears of both the Kamakura Raid and the Infinity Castle, as solo runs take significantly longer and increase the risk of failure.
