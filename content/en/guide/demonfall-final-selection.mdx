<Section icon={Target} title="Understanding the Final Selection Trial">

The **demonfall final selection** is the most critical milestone for any aspiring warrior in the world of Demonfall. Located in a specialized challenge area, this trial serves as the gateway between being a mere human and becoming a formal member of the Slayer Corps. Within the dense, wisteria-surrounded forest, players must test their combat prowess against various demonic entities to prove their worth.

In the current 2026 meta, the trial remains a gatekeeper for progression. If you fail, you remain a Ronin or must reset your character to try a different path. Success, however, grants you the tools needed to hunt higher-tier bosses and participate in endgame raids. The environment is dark and treacherous, requiring players to manage not just their health, but also their stamina and hunger bars simultaneously.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Preparation and Requirements">

Success in the **demonfall final selection** begins long before you enter the forest. Most players rush in at Level 5 with no items, only to be overwhelmed by the sheer number of demons. Proper preparation involves visiting **Hayakawa Village** or **Okuyia Village** to gather resources and potentially rolling for a beneficial Clan buff.

Your choice of Clan can drastically alter the difficulty of the selection. For instance, the **Kamado** clan provides a massive stamina boost, while the **Tokito** clan allows for faster experience gain. Even if you do not have a legendary clan, you should focus on mastering the basic M1 (Light Attack) and M2 (Heavy Attack) combos with your starting wooden katana.

| Preparation Category | Recommended Items/Status |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Consumables** | 5x Soup, 3x Bandages |
| **Stat Investment** | 2 points in Strength, 2 points in Vitality |
| **Combat Skill** | Master the "Block-Counter" technique |
| **Clan Priority** | Any clan with Health or Stamina buffs |
| **Economy** | At least 100 Yen for emergency supplies |

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Selection Walkthrough">

Navigating the **demonfall final selection** requires a methodical approach. The forest is vast, and getting lost can lead you into "hot zones" where multiple demons spawn at once. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth transition into the Slayer Corps.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Selection NPC">
    Travel to the Final Selection Area entrance. You will find two NPCs standing near the wisteria trees. Talk to them to confirm you meet the Level 5 requirement and initiate the teleport.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish a Perimeter">
    Once inside, do not run into the center of the forest. Stay near the edges where spawns are more predictable. This allows you to pull demons one by one rather than fighting a crowd.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The 30-Kill Grind">
    Engage demons using the M1-M1-M1-M2 combo. Always hold your block (F key) immediately after a combo ends. You need 30 confirmed kills to trigger the completion phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Resources">
    Every 10 kills, retreat to a safe tree or rock to consume Soup. If your health is low, use a Bandage to stop any bleeding effects caused by powerful demon strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Exit through the Gate">
    Once the counter hits 30, a notification will appear. Head back to the starting gate (marked by glowing lanterns) to exit the trial and receive your rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
Lure demons toward the Wisteria trees if you are being chased by too many. Demons take passive damage and eventually disintegrate when they get too close to the Wisteria border, giving you a much-needed breather.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Enemy Types and Combat Tactics">

The demons found within the **demonfall final selection** vary in strength and ability. While most are "Generic Demons," you may occasionally encounter faster variants that utilize basic Blood Demon Arts or heavy lunging attacks. Understanding their patterns is the difference between victory and a long walk back to the village.

| Demon Type | Danger Level | Combat Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weak Demon** | Low | Standard M1 combos, easy to stun-lock. |
| **Fast Demon** | Medium | Wait for their lunge, dodge left, then counter. |
| **Heavy Demon** | High | Never trade hits. Block their M2, then punish. |
| **Blue Demon** | Very High | Rare spawn. Use heavy knockback to keep distance. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Tip">
Always keep an eye on your Stamina bar (the blue one). If you run out of stamina, you cannot block or dash, leaving you completely vulnerable to a demon's finishing move.
</Callout>
