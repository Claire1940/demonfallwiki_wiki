<Section icon={Swords} title="Obtaining Breathing Styles and Final Selection">

Before you can officially take on the main **demonfall quests**, you must prove your worth by mastering a Breathing Style. This is a multi-step process that involves heavy grinding and a challenging duel against a trainer. Most trainers are located in hidden or specific dojos, such as the one in Okia Village. For example, Wind Breathing is highly recommended for PVE due to its massive Area of Effect (AOE), allowing you to clear groups of demons with ease.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect 20 Demon Horns">
    Travel to the Frosty Cavern. This location is the most efficient spot to farm demons. You need 20 horns to even speak with most trainers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Trainer">
    For Wind Breathing, head to Okia Village and find the dojo. Each style has a unique location, so consult your map frequently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Trainer">
    This is a 1v1 duel. Trainers often jump and use high-mobility skills. Bring a friend if the game allows, or focus on M1 combos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter Final Selection">
    Once you have your breathing style, enter the Final Selection trial. You must survive and kill a specific number of demons to earn your Slayer Nichirin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| **Yen** | Varies | Any Village | Used to purchase gear and resets |
| **Level** | 10+ | Any | Minimum recommended for Final Selection |

<Section icon={Target} title="Core Storyline Quests: Lower Moon Bosses">

After surviving Final Selection and receiving your Nichirin sword, you return to Hayah to begin the official **demonfall quests**. The first major hurdle is Lower Moon 6, Okuro. These missions transition from simple demon hunting to complex boss encounters that require specific mechanics to overcome. Following Okuro, you will be directed to Ubuyashiki, who oversees the higher-stakes missions involving the Twelve Kizuki.

| Quest Name | Boss / Objective | Strategy | Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mission 1** | **Okuro (LM6)** | Use the "High Ground" bug; spam M1 from above. | Slayer Rank Up, XP, Yen |
| **Mission 2** | **Kill 8 Demons** | Farm the outskirts of Hayakawa for fast kills. | Progression to Rui Quest |
| **Mission 3** | **Rui (LM5)** | Avoid the threads; use long-range breathing skills. | High XP, Rare Drops |
| **Mission 4** | **Lower Moon 2** | Parry the heavy strikes; stay mobile to avoid AOE. | Access to Infinite Castle |

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Strategy">
For Okuro, there is a known tactic where you can stand on a specific elevated part of the arena. He will often glitch, allowing you to deal free damage without taking hits.
</Callout>

The transition from Mission 2 to Mission 3 is a significant difficulty spike. You will need to move from basic demon hunting to the Rui mission, which requires better timing and resource management. Ensure your breathing bar is always upgraded before attempting these.

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Infinite Castle: Final Storyline Challenge">

The pinnacle of the currently available **demonfall quests** is the Infinite Castle mission. This is an endgame gauntlet that pits you against the most powerful entities in the game: the Top 3 Upper Moons. Unlike previous missions, this requires a high level of mastery over your breathing style and perfect parry timing. You will face Akaza, Doma, and Kokushibo in sequence, making this the ultimate test of a Slayer's resolve.

**Upper Moon Encounter Details:**
- **Akaza**: Focuses on brutal hand-to-hand combat. His speed is his greatest weapon.
- **Doma**: Uses ice-based AOE attacks that can freeze you in place. Maintaining distance is key.
- **Kokushibo**: The most dangerous boss, utilizing Moon Breathing with massive range and damage.

| Boss | Difficulty | Primary Threat | Recommended Style |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Akaza** | ★★★★☆ | Rapid-fire strikes / Shockwaves | Thunder or Water |
| **Doma** | ★★★★☆ | Freezing AOE / Clones | Wind or Mist |
| **Kokushibo** | ★★★★★ | Massive Range / Instant Breaks | Sun or Flame |

<Callout type="success" title="Endgame Achievement">
Completing the Infinite Castle mission marks the end of the current Slayer storyline. This grants massive prestige and unlocks high-tier gear options.
</Callout>
