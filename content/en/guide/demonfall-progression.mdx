The journey in Demonfall starts immediately upon spawning into the world. Your first encounter is with a demon commonly referred to by the community as Charles. This fight is the most critical moment in your early **demonfall progression**, as the outcome dictates your starting race and the subsequent storyline you will follow.

<Section icon={Zap} title="Early Game Leveling and Questing">

Once your race is established, your primary goal is to reach the Final Selection (for Slayers) or master your first Blood Demon Art (for Demons). For Slayers, you will find yourself in Hayakawa Village. You must speak with Hayaka, who serves as your primary quest giver for the initial stages of the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Training Phase">
    Talk to Hayaka and complete the initial delivery and combat missions. This will get you to level 10-15 quickly, allowing you to seek out a Breathing Trainer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Learning a Breathing Style">
    Locate a trainer for styles like Water, Flame, or Wind. Each requires a specific quest (usually involving parkour or resource gathering) and a fee of 2,000 Yen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Final Selection">
    Once you reach level 20, travel to the Final Selection area. You must survive for seven days (in-game time) and defeat a specific number of demons to officially join the Slayer Corps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For Demons, the **demonfall progression** is slightly more streamlined. You will interact with an NPC in the demon cave who provides a shorter, more combat-intensive storyline. Your focus will be on consuming souls to increase your power and talking to the BDA NPC to roll for your specific demonic abilities.

| Activity | XP Yield | Level Requirement | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crow Quests** | Low-Medium | Level 1+ | Solo early leveling |
| **Final Selection** | High (One-time) | Level 20 | Unlocking Slayer rank |
| **Blue Demon Boss** | Medium | Level 15+ | Early group farming |
| **Story Missions** | High | Variable | Core progression |

<Callout type="warning" title="Quest Efficiency">
Avoid the "Hunt Demons" quests from the Crow. These often require finding specific demons across the map, which takes significantly longer than simply farming static boss spawns or completing story objectives.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Mid-Game Boss Farming Strategies">

The transition from level 30 to 50 is where many players feel the grind. To optimize your **demonfall progression**, you should stop relying on standard quests and shift your focus toward world bosses. The most popular targets in 2026 remain Uto and Kaaku.

Farming these bosses is significantly more efficient when done in a group. Using the official Discord server to find "Help Requests" allows you to join servers where these bosses have already spawned.

| Boss Name | Location | Notable Drops | XP Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Uto** | Various Spawns | Demon Parts, Ore | Very High |
| **Kaaku** | Various Spawns | Rare Crafting Mats | High |
| **Zenitsu** | Coastline | Thunder Essence | Medium-High |
| **Giyuu** | Snowy Forest | Water Essence | Medium-High |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="End-Game: Raids and the Prestige System">

The true end-game of Demonfall revolves around the Prestige system. Reaching the level cap is only the beginning. By talking to specific NPCs, you can Prestige, which resets your level to 1 but grants you a permanent increase in level cap and additional skill points.

To reach the level required for Prestige quickly, Raids are the absolute meta. However, Raids in Kamakura Village typically require you to be Prestige 5 to enter the most rewarding tiers.

<Callout type="info" title="Raids Explanation">
Raids are wave-based survival modes. If you enter with a party, the entire group is teleported. Successfully clearing all waves provides the single highest XP payout in the game, often jumping a player several levels in a single run.

<Section icon={Coins} title="Economic Progression: Farming Yen Fast">

Money (Yen) is vital for buying potions, reset items, and upgrading weapons. While quests give small amounts, the fastest way to build wealth in 2026 is crystal farming in the Okia Cave. This cave is located just outside Okia Village and contains a specific climbing section that leads to high-value item spawns.

| Item | Sell Price (Yen) | Rarity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect Crystal** | 6,000 | Rare |
| **Green Crystal** | 3,000 | Uncommon |
| **Trinkets** | 200 - 1,000 | Common |
| **Demon Horns** | 100 | Common |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Travel to Okia Village">
    Set your spawn at Okia Village for easy access.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Cave">
    Locate the Okia Cave and use the "weird" climbing path to reach the upper ledge where crystals spawn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Server Hopping">
    Pick up all crystals and trinkets, then immediately swap servers. You will spawn back in the same spot, allowing you to check for new spawns instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Crystal Efficiency">
Don't ignore the smaller trinkets. While Perfect Crystals are the goal, a bag full of common trinkets can still net you 2,000-3,000 Yen per run, making the process consistent even when luck is low.
</Callout>

</Section>
