<Section icon={Swords} title="Choosing Your Path: Slayer vs. Demon">

Immediately after character customization, you are thrown into a playable tutorial fight against a demon. The outcome of this fight determines your race and the entire trajectory of your gameplay.

If you defeat the demon using your basic rusty katana, you will remain human and proceed to the Demon Slayer path. To win this fight, use basic light attack (M1) combos and heavy attacks (M2) to break the demon's guard when it blocks. If you are struggling, use your dash-flip to create distance and wait for an opening.

If you let the demon defeat you, your character will transform into a Demon. This path grants you unique supernatural abilities called Blood Demon Arts but makes you vulnerable to sunlight and Nichirin blades. 

| Aspect | Slayer Path | Demon Path |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Core Weapon** | Nichirin Katana (forged after exam) | Fists, Claws, and Blood Demon Arts |
| **Power Source** | Breathing Styles (Wind, Flame, Thunder, etc.) | Blood Demon Arts & Demon Skills |
| **Environmental Hazards** | Standard fall damage and enemy attacks | Severe damage when exposed to sunlight |
| **Progression Goal** | Complete Final Selection, join Slayer Corps | Consume human souls, defeat Slayers |
| **Leveling Difficulty** | Moderate (requires questing & gourds) | Harder early game, high PvP potential |

<Section icon={Target} title="Passing the Final Selection Exam">

To become an official member of the Demon Slayer Corps, you must travel to the Sakura Passage and complete the Final Selection exam. This exam requires you to survive in a demon-infested forest and defeat 30 demons.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Inventory">
    Before heading to the exam, travel to Okuya Village and visit the local shop. Purchase plenty of bread. Hunger drains quickly, and having food is vital for passive health regeneration during the long exam.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Demon Horn Quest">
    Talk to the Wind Trainer located just outside Okuya Village. He will task you with collecting 20 demon horns. By grabbing this quest before the exam, the 30 demons you kill during the selection will automatically drop the horns you need.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate to Sakura Passage">
    Follow the path to the right side of Okuya Village until you see the sign for Sakura Passage. Walk through this area to reach the Slayer Exam gates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat 30 Demons Safely">
    Once inside, focus on pulling 1-2 standard demons at a time. Use basic M1 strings. If a demon blocks, use a dash-flip to reset or execute a heavy right-click attack to break their guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Forge Your Nichirin Blade">
    After surviving the exam, talk to the forge NPC to craft your custom Nichirin Katana using the Sun Ore rewarded to you. The blade color is randomized but can be rerolled later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Enemy Type | Danger Level | Key Attacks | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Demon** | Low | Basic claws, block-heavy AI | M1 spam, right-click block break, flip away |
| **Shadow Demon** | Medium | Fast dash attacks, quick combos | Perfect block their initial dash, counter-attack |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Breathing Styles & Gourd Training">

Breathing Styles are the core combat systems for Slayers. To unlock and use your breathing skills effectively, you must train your lungs using gourds. This process is known as Gourd Training.

You can purchase gourds from the merchant in Okuya Village for 500 Yen each. To train, equip the gourd and click your mouse to blow into it. A mini-game will appear where you must keep a progress bar in the safe middle zone. If you let the bar fill into the red zone, the gourd will pop, wasting your investment.

Your breathing level starts at 0 and maxes out at level 10. As you break gourds, your breathing level increases, allowing you to execute more powerful skills without running out of breath.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Early Game Leveling & Progression Routes">

Once you have your Breathing Style and Nichirin Blade, your primary goal is to level up, earn Yen, and unlock active skills on your skill tree.

To earn Yen quickly in the early game, search the ground in villages for valuable trinkets to sell to merchants. You can also purchase a pickaxe to mine ore veins scattered around the mountainous regions.

For experience points, turn on your Crow in the settings menu. The Crow will periodically assign you extermination and delivery quests that yield substantial EXP and Yen. While some players attempt the Okuro Dungeon (Lower Moon 6) early on, it is highly buggy and not recommended for solo beginners.
