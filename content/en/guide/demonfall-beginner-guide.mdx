<Section icon={Map} title="The First Steps: Slayer or Demon?">

Once you enter the game, you are immediately thrown into a tutorial combat scenario. This is the most critical fork in the road for any player. Your performance here determines your race.

To become a **Slayer**, you must defeat the tutorial demon using the basic katana provided. This requires basic mastery of the M1 (left-click) attack strings and blocking. If the demon starts blocking, use a heavy attack (right-click) to break its guard.

To become a **Demon**, you simply need to let the tutorial demon kill you. This will teleport you to a different starting area where you will begin your life as a creature of the night.

| Path | Difficulty | Key Advantage | Main Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slayer** | Moderate | Access to Breathing Styles | Passing Final Selection |
| **Demon** | Easy Start | High Natural Regen, Blood Arts | Devouring Souls |
| **Hybrid** | Very Hard | Combines both powers | Late-game evolution |

After completing the tutorial, follow the path to **Hayakawa Village**. This serves as your initial hub where you will meet your Uncle and receive your first instructions.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
Do not wander too far into high-level zones early on. Stick to the paths and look for signs leading to Okuya Village, as it is the most prosperous village near the Final Selection area.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Final Selection">

For those who chose the path of the Slayer, the **Final Selection** is the ultimate rite of passage. You cannot become a true Demon Slayer or obtain a Nichirin Blade without passing this exam. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation">
    Travel to **Okuya Village**. Purchase bread for healing and ensure you have at least 100 Yen for fast travel if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gathering Horns">
    Locate your desired Breathing Trainer. Most trainers, such as the Wind Trainer, require you to bring **20 Demon Horns**. It is highly efficient to gather these *during* the Final Selection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sakura Passage">
    Follow the signs to the **Sakura Passage**. This area leads directly to the Slayer Exam. Do not go past the exam entrance until you are ready to fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Exam">
    You must survive and kill **30 Demons** within the forest. Avoid the **Blue Demon** at all costs; it is an optional boss that is extremely tanky and offers no significant reward for beginners.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Target | HP Level | Strategy | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common Demon** | Low | M1 Spam, Dash away if they block | High |
| **Blue Demon** | Very High | **Avoid entirely** | None |
| **Demon Groups** | Variable | Use AOE skills or kite them | Medium |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Breathing Styles and Training Mechanics">

Breathing is the core power system for Slayers. After passing the Final Selection, you will receive a **Nichirin Blade**. The color is random but can be rerolled using Robux or specific in-game items. However, the blade is useless without a Breathing Style.

**Breathing Training (Gourds):**
To maximize your breathing techniques, you must train your lung capacity. This is done by purchasing **Gourds** from the shop in Okuya Village.

| Gourd Type | Cost | Level Increase | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Small Gourd** | 500 Yen | Level 0 to 5 | Basic Breathing |
| **Medium Gourd** | 700 Yen | Level 5 to 7 | Intermediate |
| **Large Gourd** | 1,000 Yen | Level 7 to 10 | Advanced |

Training involves a mini-game where you must keep a bar centered. If the bar hits the red zones, the training fails. Breaking a gourd successfully increases your breathing level, allowing you to use skills more frequently without running out of breath.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Economy: Farming Yen and Resources">

Yen is the lifeblood of your progression. You need it for Gourds, food, fast travel, and forging. For a beginner, there are three primary ways to earn money efficiently.

1.  **Trinket Hunting**: Search the ground in villages and forests. Trinkets like rings and necklaces can be sold to shopkeepers for quick cash.
2.  **Crow Missions**: Once you become a Slayer, your Crow will periodically offer missions. These are usually "Kill X Demons" and provide both EXP and Yen.
3.  **Mining**: Purchase a pickaxe and find ore veins. This is slower but provides consistent income and materials for crafting.

| Item | Sell Value | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Broken Ring** | 20 Yen | Ground Spawn (Common) |
| **Gold Necklace** | 100 Yen | Ground Spawn (Rare) |
| **Demon Horn** | 10 Yen | Demon Drops |
| **Ore (Various)** | 15-50 Yen | Caves/Rock Outcrops |

<Callout type="warning" title="Death Penalty">
Be aware that dying in a PvP-enabled server or certain combat zones will cause you to lose a percentage of your current Yen. Always spend your money on Gourds or items before heading into dangerous territory.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Progression: Bosses and Dungeons">

Once you have your Breathing Style and have reached Level 10-15, you can attempt boss missions. One of the first major challenges is the **Okuro Boss Mission**, accessible through the lady in Hayakawa Village.

Okuro is a former Lower Moon 6 demon who uses fire-based attacks. This fight is notoriously difficult to solo for beginners because his fire breath and stomps can fling players across the map, sometimes resulting in a "kick" from the server due to anti-cheat glitches.

**Boss Strategy:**
- **Don't Solo**: Bring at least one friend. Use the "Squad" button at the top left to party up.
- **Block the Stomps**: You can block his physical kicks, but you must run away from the fire breath.
- **Stay Mobile**: Use your breathing dash skills to stay behind him.
