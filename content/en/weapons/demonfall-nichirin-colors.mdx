<Section icon={Target} title="How to Reroll and Change Sword Colors">

If you are unsatisfied with the color you received during the initial forging process, you can change it. The Forge Master offers color customization services in exchange for in-game currency or premium points.

| Method | Cost | Speed | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Yen Reroll | 3,000 Yen | Slow (requires farming) | Recommended for F2P players |
| Robux Reroll | 50 Robux | Instant | Recommended for fast adjustments |

Farming the required Yen involves completing daily quests, defeating bosses, and selling valuable trinkets to merchants. Players who prefer not to spend Robux should focus on optimizing their Yen farming routes.
