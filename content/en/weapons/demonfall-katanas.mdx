<Section icon={Swords} title="Demonfall Katanas and Swordsmanship Overview">

In the dark, demon-infested world of Roblox Demonfall, your weapon is the only barrier between survival and a swift demise. For those walking the path of the Demon Slayer Corps, **demonfall katanas** (known lore-wise as Nichirin Swords) are the ultimate tools of purification. These specialized blades are forged from unique ore that absorbs sunlight, making them the only weapons capable of permanently slaying demons. 

Understanding how your sword functions is critical to mastering the game's combat loop. Melee combat relies on a combination of Light Attacks (LMB) and Heavy Attacks (RMB). Light attacks allow you to execute swift, fluid combos that keep opponents staggered, while heavy attacks deal massive guard-break damage at the cost of longer recovery frames. Your choice of katana directly influences your base damage output, which then scales dynamically with your active Breathing Style and passive skill tree investments.

| Weapon Type | Base Damage | Purchase Cost | Forging Requirements | Best Combat Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Rusty Katana | 4 | Free | Starter Weapon | Early Leveling |
| Slayer Katana | 5 | 150 Yen | None | Mid-Game Slayer |
| Custom Katana | 6 | 1,000 Yen | Breath Style Mastery | Late-Game Builds |

Choosing when to transition from your basic starter gear to a specialized weapon is a milestone that every player must face. While the Rusty Katana is sufficient for clearing out weak spirits in Hayakawa Village, attempting to tackle high-tier bosses like Rui or Akaza with a low-tier blade will result in rapid defeat.

<Callout type="info" title="Damage Scaling Note">
Base sword damage is only the foundation of your offensive power. Investing skill points into the Sword Mastery node in your character skill tree will further amplify the damage of all light and heavy attacks.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Forge and Upgrade Your Katana">

To truly unlock the power of **demonfall katanas**, you must upgrade your standard Slayer weapon into a Custom Breathing Katana. This process requires you to gather rare materials scattered across the map and locate the master blacksmith. The custom forging system is designed to reward players who actively explore dangerous territories and defeat powerful demon threats.

Before you can forge a custom weapon, you must ensure that you have fully mastered your chosen Breathing Style. The blacksmith will refuse to work on your blade if you have not unlocked the corresponding elemental techniques in your skill tree.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pass the Final Selection">
    Travel to the Final Selection Area and survive the demon-infested forest trial. Completing this challenge unlocks the Slayer Corps Base and grants you your initial Slayer Katana.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Forging Materials">
    Explore the mountain caves to mine Sun Ore. You will also need to hunt down rogue demons in the wild to collect Demon Horns, which act as a binding agent for the elemental forge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Blacksmith">
    Navigate to the Slayer Corps Base and locate the master blacksmith NPC near the central forge. Ensure you have your materials and the required Yen ready in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Forge Your Custom Blade">
    Interact with the blacksmith, choose your desired elemental upgrade path, and pay the forging fee. Your standard katana will be transformed into a powerful custom weapon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Farming the materials required for this upgrade can be dangerous. Use the table below to plan your farming routes and understand where to find each essential item:

| Forging Material | Primary Source Location | Average Drop Rate | Primary Upgrade Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Sun Ore | Mountain Caves (Mining Veins) | 100% via Mining | Core component for all custom blades |
| Demon Horn | Defeating Wild Demons | 35% Drop Chance | Required for elemental binding |
| Yen | Completing Quests & Boss Hunts | 100% Reward | Forging and purchasing service fee |

<Callout type="success" title="Farming Tip">
When mining Sun Ore in the caves, bring a friend to watch your back. Demons frequently spawn in dark areas, and getting ambushed while locked in a mining animation can easily lead to a lost life.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Best Katana Builds and Breathing Synergy">

A custom katana is only as strong as the build supporting it. To maximize your combat efficiency in both PvP encounters and PvE boss farming, you must coordinate your weapon choice, Breathing Style, and skill tree allocation. Certain styles gain massive passive benefits when paired with their corresponding custom weapon designs.

For example, players utilizing Sun Breathing will find that the legendary Black Katana not only looks incredibly striking but also perfectly complements the high-damage, wide-sweeping strikes of the Sun style. Meanwhile, Mist Breathing users benefit heavily from the stealth and mobility options provided by a lightweight custom blade.

When allocating your skill points, prioritize unlocking your Breathing Style's active skills before dumping remaining points into raw Sword Mastery. Having access to a diverse kit of moves is far more valuable than a minor boost to your basic light attacks.

<Callout type="tip" title="Build Optimization">
