<Section icon={Map} title="Crafting Materials and Boss Locations">

Crafting the most powerful **demonfall swords** requires more than just Yen. You will need to hunt down specific bosses scattered across the map to obtain rare reagents. Each high-tier blade has a unique recipe that often involves a mix of common ores and legendary drops. 

The most difficult material to obtain is the Crystal Essence, which requires you to craft a Crystal Key and venture into a hidden cave outside of Okuya. This boss is a significant gear check for new players, so ensure your skill tree is properly invested before attempting the encounter.

| Material | Primary Source | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sun Ore** | Mining with Pickaxe | Any Cave (10 required) |
| **Demon Horn** | Regular Demons | Various (10 required) |
| **Green Horn** | Green Demon Boss | Cave outside Hayakawa |
| **Demon Collar** | Demon Axe Boss | Wall area near Mitsuri |
| **Crystal Essence** | Crystal Boss | Hidden Cave near Okuya |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mining Tip">
Always purchase a pickaxe from the Hayakawa vendor before heading out. Mining 10 Sun Ore is a prerequisite for almost every custom Nichirin blade, and you don't want to find a vein without the proper tools.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Specialty Blades: Unique Mechanics">

Some **demonfall swords** don't just hit harder; they change how you play the game. The Sound and Insect blades, for example, feature unique animation sets that differ from the standard katana swings. These weapons often hit multiple times per click, making them excellent for breaking guards or applying pressure.

The Stone Flail is another outlier. Unlike every other Nichirin in the game, it cannot be sheathed. It is a massive axe and flail combination that trades stealth and speed for consistent, heavy-hitting pressure. While currently ranked in C-Tier due to its basic +50% buff, it remains a favorite for players who prefer a "juggernaut" playstyle.
