<Section icon={Flame} title="Fundamental Breathing Styles: Requirements and Stats">

The fundamental styles are the backbone of the Slayer Corps. These are generally easier to acquire but remain highly effective even in late-game scenarios. Most require a visit to a specific trainer located in the snowy peaks or hidden villages.

| Style | Trainer Location | Cost (Yen) | Primary Items Needed | Combat Focus |
|-------|------------------|------------|----------------------|--------------|
| **Flame** | White Peak | 0 | 20 Demon Horns, Axe Collar | Burst Damage |
| **Water** | Coast Forest | 2,000 | None (Obstacle Course) | Defense/Combos |
| **Thunder** | Coast House | 4,000 | 20 Soups, Letter Delivery | Speed/Mobility |
| **Wind** | Near HQ | 0 | 20 Demon Horns, Duel | Crowd Control |
| **Stone** | High Peak | 20,000 | 50 Demon Horns | Raw Power/Stun |

**Flame Breathing** remains a fan favorite due to its high burn damage. Rengoku, the trainer at White Peak, tasks you with slaying demons and the Axe Demon before granting you the Flame Tiger and Rising Sun forms. **Water Breathing**, taught by Urokodaki, focuses on fluidity. You must navigate a trap-filled mountain path and slice a massive boulder to prove your worth.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced and Specialized Breathing Styles">

Advanced **demonfall breathing styles** often incorporate status effects like poison or unique weapon types. These styles usually demand more effort to unlock, such as finding multiple NPCs or rare trinkets.

| Style | Special Mechanic | Unlock Challenge | Best For |
|-------|------------------|------------------|----------|
| **Mist** | Obscured Vision | Navigate the Mist Maze | PvP Confusion |
| **Insect** | Poison Damage | 10,000 Yen Payment | Fast M1 Attacks |
| **Beast** | Dual Katanas | 100 Meat, Blue/Green Horns | Aggressive Rush |
| **Sound** | Explosive Aura | Find 3 Wives, 3 Crystals | Area Damage |
| **Flower** | Kinetic Vision | Find 3 Unique Flowers | Counter-Attacking |

**Insect Breathing** is unique because it relies on poison stacks rather than raw physical damage. This makes it lethal against high-HP bosses. **Sound Breathing**, taught by Uzui, requires you to track down his three wives across the map. It offers the "Chain Explosion" move, which is excellent for clearing groups of enemies.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Pinnacle Styles: Sun and Moon">

Sun and Moon are considered the ultimate **demonfall breathing styles**. They possess the highest damage scaling and the most visually impressive effects, but their requirements are the strictest in the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level Thresholds">
    For Moon Breathing, you must reach Level 50 and possess at least Prestige 2. Sun Breathing requires a similar level of dedication and typically a human race.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Rare Resources">
    Moon Breathing requires Muzan Blood and a conversation with Kokushibo. Sun Breathing requires finding Tanjiro near the coast.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Ritual">
    Interact with the respective trainers. For Moon, you must accept Kokushibo's deal, which transforms you into a Hybrid/Demon if you weren't one already.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master the Forms">
    Sun Breathing features moves like "Dragon Sun Halo Head Dance," while Moon focuses on "Catastrophe" and "Dark Moon" slashes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Prestige Guide">
