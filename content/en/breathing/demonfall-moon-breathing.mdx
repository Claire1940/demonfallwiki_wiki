To successfully transition your character and obtain Moon Breathing, you must fulfill several conditions. The table below details the exact prerequisites required to complete the process.

| Requirement | Target Value | Purpose |

<Section icon={Target} title="Becoming a Hybrid (Oni) Step-by-Step">

Transitioning into a Hybrid is the most resource-intensive phase of obtaining demonfall moon breathing. Hybrids enjoy the unique benefit of utilizing breathing techniques while possessing demon regeneration and physical buffs. To initiate this transformation, players must acquire Muzan Blood, a rare and highly sought-after commodity in the Demonfall universe. 

The Mysterious Merchant is your primary source for this item, spawning dynamically in key residential zones.

### Mysterious Merchant Locations

Finding the merchant requires patience, as he rotates between specific village centers. Check these locations regularly to purchase your required items.

| Village | Spawn Location | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hayakawa Village** | Near the village center | Look near the market stalls and central pathways. |
| **Okuya Village** | Central plaza area | Spawns close to the main administrative buildings. |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 50 and Prestige 1">
    Grind your character to Level 50 after prestiging at least once. This unlocks the dialogue options required for the transformation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire 5 Muzan Bloods">
    Locate the Mysterious Merchant in Hayakawa or Okuya Village. Purchase 5 Muzan Bloods using your accumulated Yen or through player trading networks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Kokushibo Trainer">
    Travel to the snowy mountain peaks. Climb the mountain path until you locate Kokushibo resting near the cliff edge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consume the Blood and Transform">
    Speak to Kokushibo while holding the 5 Muzan Bloods. Accept his offer to drink the blood, which will instantly transform your character into a Hybrid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Finding the Moon Breathing Trainer Kokushibo">

The trainer for Moon Breathing is Kokushibo, the formidable Upper Moon One. Unlike standard trainers who reside in easily accessible towns, Kokushibo is hidden deep within the treacherous, snow-capped mountain ranges of the map. Reaching him requires navigating vertical cliffs and harsh winter environments, making preparation essential before setting out.

To reach the trainer, start from the main mountain entrance and follow the ascending path upward. Keep the cliffside to your left as you climb. You will need to utilize double jumps and wall climbs to scale several steep rock faces. Once you reach the upper plateau, look for a secluded clearing shielded by rock formations; Kokushibo will be waiting there, recognizable by his distinct multi-eyed appearance and traditional samurai armor.

<Callout type="info" title="Navigation Guide">
If you get lost in the snowstorm, look for the glowing lanterns along the mountain paths. These serve as checkpoints and indicate that you are heading in the correct direction toward the peak.
</Callout>
