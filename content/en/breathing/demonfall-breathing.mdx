<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Maze Walkthrough">

Navigating the path to Mist Breathing is the hardest part of the acquisition process. The maze is tight and narrow, requiring precise movements. Use the following sequence to reach the end without getting lost.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Entrance">
    Enter the maze and immediately take the first **Left**, then proceed **Straight** down the main corridor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Junction">
    Turn **Left**, then immediately **Right**. Continue straight until you see the next opening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Mid-Section">
    Execute a **Right** turn followed by a **Left**. Repeat this (**Right then Left**) one more time to clear the zig-zag area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Long Corridor">
    After the zig-zags, take a **Right** and go all the way down the long path. Ignore the side openings until you reach the end.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Stretch">
    Turn **Left**, ignore the first narrow path, and take the **second narrow opening**. This leads directly to Tokito's clearing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Oxygen Mastery">

Once you have unlocked your breathing style, you must learn to manage your "Oxygen Bar." Every breathing move consumes a portion of this bar. If the bar empties, you will be unable to use skills and your character will enter a fatigued state, leaving you vulnerable to attacks.

**Key Combat Controls:**
- **Hold G**: Refills the breathing bar. This should be done during gaps in enemy attack patterns.
- **Left Click (M1)**: Basic sword strikes that build a small amount of meter.
- **Skill Keys (Z, X, C, V, B)**: Execute specific breathing techniques.

| Move Name | Description | Bar Consumption |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cloudy Seas** | A wide AOE slash that knocks back enemies | 25% |
| **Eight Layered Mist** | Multiple rapid slashes in front of the user | 40% |
| **Distant Fog** | A dash attack that closes the gap instantly | 30% |
| **Obscuring Clouds** | Creates a zone that hides the user's movement | 50% |

<Callout type="tip" title="PvP Tip">
In PvP, never let your breathing bar drop below 20%. Always keep enough "breath" to use a defensive or escape move like Distant Fog if your opponent catches you in a combo.
</Callout>

</Section>
