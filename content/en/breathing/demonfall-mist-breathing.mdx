<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the Mist Maze Walkthrough">

The Mist Maze is notorious for its confusing layout and invisible barriers. Many players struggle here because the path is not visually intuitive. Following a specific sequence of turns is the only way to reach the center where the Mist Breathing style is granted.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Entry">
    Enter the maze and immediately take a **Left**, then proceed straight until you hit the first barrier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Mid-Section">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Narrow Corridor">
    Continue through a series of quick turns: **Left**, **Right**, **Left**, and **Right**. You will eventually reach a narrow path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Stretch">
    Go all the way down the longest corridor, ignore the first side opening, and enter the **skinniest narrow path** on the left to reach the end.
  </Step>

| Movement Step | Direction | Action |

| **Step 7-10** | Left -> Right -> Left -> Right | Entering the zigzag section |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Mist Breathing Moveset and Combat Strategy">

Demonfall Mist Breathing is categorized by its ability to confuse opponents. By utilizing "obscuring" mechanics, Mist users can make it difficult for enemies to predict their next move. This makes the style particularly potent in PvP (Player vs. Player) scenarios.

**Core Combat Mechanics:**
- **Oxygen Bar**: Like all breathing styles, Mist requires oxygen. Hold **G** to breathe and refill your meter.
- **Agility Focus**: Mist moves often involve dashes or teleports, allowing you to close gaps or escape combos.
- **Visual Distraction**: Several moves create mist clouds that hide your character's animations.

| Move Name | Type | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Obscuring Clouds** | Utility/Dash | Creates a cloud and allows for a quick reposition |
| **Eight Layered Mist** | Multi-hit | A series of rapid slashes in a small area |
| **Distant Horizon** | Range/AOE | A sweeping strike that hits enemies at a distance |
| **Lunar Dispersing Mist** | Defensive | A counter-like move that punishes aggressive players |

Mist Breathing scales primarily with your **Strength** and **Breathing** stats. To maximize damage, players should focus on upgrading their breathing bar capacity, allowing for longer chains of techniques without needing to pause and recharge.

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Strategy">
In PvP, use Obscuring Clouds to bait out an opponent's dash. Once they have exhausted their stamina, follow up with Eight Layered Mist for maximum damage.
</Callout>
