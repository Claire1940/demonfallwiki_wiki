<Section icon={Target} title="Surviving the Water Trainer's Parkour Obstacle Course">

The parkour trial is the most challenging phase of obtaining **demonfall water breathing**. The mountain cave is littered with spike traps, swinging logs, and hidden tripwires that can instantly kill unprepared players. Success requires patience, strict stamina management, and understanding the layout of each trap sector.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stamina Preservation">
    Walk or use single jumps over the initial rope bridges. Avoid double-jumping unnecessarily to keep your stamina bar full for critical leaps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Horizontal Rope Transitions">
    Execute precise horizontal jumps across the suspended ropes. Walk off the ledge slightly before jumping to ensure clean contact with the next platform.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spotting Hidden Tripwires">
    Point your camera downward while traversing grassy areas. This reveals the wire locations, allowing you to jump over them safely without triggering spikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Dash Jump">
    Double-tap W to sprint, then press Space and Q simultaneously to clear the large gap leading to the trainer's platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Do not rush through the course. Most failures occur because players attempt to speedrun the obstacles and deplete their stamina, leading to missed jumps and fatal falls.

| Trap Name | Damage Type | Detection Method | Avoidance Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spike Pit** | Instant Death | Visible gaps in wooden flooring | Single jump with Q-dash mid-air |
| **Ground Spikes** | Heavy Damage | Hidden under tall grass patches | Tilt camera down, jump over wires |
| **Swinging Log** | Knockback & Fall | Auditory creaking sounds | Pause before crossing narrow beams |
| **Double Tripwire** | High Damage | Two parallel lines across path | Jump over first, walk under second |
