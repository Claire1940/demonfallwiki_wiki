Obtaining the **Demonfall Sun Breathing** style is considered one of the ultimate milestones for any player in the game. Unlike basic breathing styles that can be acquired early on, Sun Breathing demands significant progression and dedication. The most critical barrier to entry is the prestige system, which resets your level but increases your power ceiling.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Prestige 1 Necessity**: You cannot talk to Tanjiro for training unless you have prestiged at least once.
- **Clan Neutrality**: Even members of the prestigious Kamado clan must meet the prestige requirement.
- **NPC Location**: Tanjiro is tucked away in a mountainous region near the False Bridge.

<Section icon={Map} title="Navigation: Finding Tanjiro's House">

The journey to find the Sun Breathing trainer is one of the most confusing paths for newer players. Tanjiro is located in a secluded area that isn't directly marked on the main map icons. Most players start their journey from Okuya Village or Kamakura Village. While Kamakura is closer to the prestige NPC, Okuya provides a more straightforward walking path for those who want to avoid high fall damage or complex elevator mechanics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start at Okuya Village">
    Spawn at Okuya Village and head toward the main gate. Take an immediate left once you exit the village walls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Soccer Tree Landmark">
    Follow the uphill path until you spot a distinctive tree, often referred to by the community as the "soccer tree." This marks the correct direction toward the rocky ledges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigating the Rocks">
    Jump over the small rock formations or walk around them to reach the gate leading to the **False Bridge**. This is a major transition zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mountain Parkour">
    Follow the cliff wall to the left of the path. You will need to perform basic wall jumps (parkour) to scale the mountain side until you reach a small plateau where Tanjiro resides.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Fast Travel Tip">
If you have already unlocked Kamakura Village, you can use the horse carriage to save time. However, be prepared to navigate the cliffside carefully, as the drop-off near the False Bridge can be lethal if you miss your jumps.
</Callout>

| Landmark | Visual Cue | Direction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ramen Shop** | Near Okuya entrance | Start Point |
| **Soccer Tree** | Large tree on a hill | North-West |
| **False Bridge** | Large wooden bridge | North |
| **Tanjiro's Hut** | Small campfire and NPC | Mountain Peak |

The strategy with Sun Breathing revolves around managing your stamina while keeping the pressure on your opponent. Because Sun moves consume a significant amount of breath, players often pair this style with the Kamado clan to mitigate the drain.

| Move Name | Primary Effect | Best Usage |

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Kamado Clan Advantage">

While any human character with Prestige 1 can learn Sun Breathing, the **Kamado Clan** is objectively the best choice for this specific style. In the world of Demonfall, clans provide passive buffs that can drastically alter the effectiveness of your build.

**Kamado Clan Passives:**
- **Stamina Regeneration**: Increased recovery speed, allowing for more frequent move usage.
- **Sun Breathing Buff**: A unique hidden modifier that enhances the damage of all Sun-based techniques.
- **Exp Boost**: Faster leveling, which is crucial since you need to reach high levels after every prestige.

<Callout type="info" title="Rerolling Advice">
If you are serious about a Sun Breathing build, it is highly recommended to use your clan rerolls until you hit Kamado. The difference in stamina management is night and one-day compared to other clans like Agatsuma or Tomioka.
</Callout>
