<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain Flame Breathing">

To acquire **demonfall flame breathing**, you must embark on a journey to the coldest part of the map. Unlike some styles that require complex trials, the Flame Breathing quest is relatively straightforward but requires a significant financial investment for early-game players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Resources">
    Ensure you have at least 4,000 Yen. You can farm this by completing the Slayer Exam or selling trinkets found in the environment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to White Peak">
    Navigate to the White Peak mountain. It is the large, snow-covered mountain visible from most parts of the map. Look for the path leading upward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Rengoku NPC">
    The trainer, Rengoku, is sitting near a campfire at the very top of the peak. He is easily identifiable by his iconic yellow and red hair.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Quest">
    Talk to Rengoku. He will ask for the 4,000 Yen. Once paid, he will instantly grant you the Flame Breathing style.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="PvP and PvE Strategy Guide">

Using **demonfall flame breathing** effectively requires a balance between aggression and timing. While the burn damage is passive, your active moves must be timed to catch dodges or break guards.

### PvP Tactics: The Aggressive Burn
In PvP, your goal is to keep the "Burn" status active at all times. Even if you aren't landing full combos, the chip damage prevents the opponent from regenerating stamina and health effectively.

- **Guard Breaking**: Use *Blazing Universe* against opponents who hold block. It has a heavy guard-break property that leaves them vulnerable to a full Purgatory follow-up.
- **Defensive Utility**: *Blooming Flame Undulation* is your best defensive tool. It creates a barrier of fire that can deflect projectiles and punish close-range attackers.

### PvE Tactics: Boss Melting
For PvE, especially against bosses like Akaza or Kaigaku, Flame Breathing is unparalleled.

1. **Stack Burns**: Use *Flame Tiger* to apply multiple stacks of burn damage.
2. **Kiting**: Use the mobility of *Unknowing Fire* to move behind bosses after they telegraph an attack.
3. **Ultimate Timing**: Save *Purgatory* for when the boss is stunned or in a recovery animation to ensure all hits connect.
