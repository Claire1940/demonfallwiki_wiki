<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Mastery">

Soloing bosses requires a more disciplined approach than group raiding. You cannot rely on others to draw aggro, so your positioning is paramount. Follow these steps to improve your solo performance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation & Buffs">
    Before engaging, ensure your hunger bar is full to maintain health regeneration. Consume a "Soup" or "Meat" item. If you have access to Clan buffs (like Kamado or Agatsuma), ensure they are active.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Aggro Pull">
    Approach the boss slowly. Use a ranged move (if available) to pull them away from other mobs. This prevents you from being swarmed by low-level demons while fighting the main target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Combo Loop">
    Most bosses can be "Infinite Combo'd" if timed correctly. Use a sequence of 4 M1 attacks followed by a knock-back skill. Wait for them to recover, then dash in to restart the loop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perfect Blocking">
    When a boss glows red or prepares a heavy attack, hold your block key just before impact. A "Perfect Block" will stun the boss for 2 seconds, allowing for a full skill rotation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Prestige Advantage">
Reaching Prestige 1 or higher significantly increases your base damage, making soloing mid-tier bosses like Rui much faster.
</Callout>
