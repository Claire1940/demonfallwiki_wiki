<Section icon={Map} title="Kokushibo Location Walkthrough">

Finding Kokushibo is a challenging task due to the complex terrain, hidden caves, and vertical cliffs surrounding his spawn point. The journey begins at the starter village and requires precise navigation through several key landmarks.

| Step Number | Starting Landmark | Directional Instructions | Key Visual Cues |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | Hayakawa Village Hotel | Exit hotel, turn right, and run straight | Flat dirt path leading out |
| **2** | Large Rock Boundary | Follow parallel to the rock, then turn left | High stone wall on the right |
| **3** | Snowy Hillside | Climb the small incline and stick to the right | Snow-covered ground and trees |
| **4** | Green Demon Cave | Enter the cave, stick to the left, and exit | Glowing green crystals |
| **5** | Moyai Statue | Go behind the stone head and turn left | Large Easter Island-style statue |
| **6** | Final Cliff Face | Perform a wall climb up the corner rocky ledge | Kokushibo sitting on the peak |

To navigate this route successfully, start at the Hayakawa Village hotel spawn. Exit the building, immediately take a right turn, and run straight until you encounter a massive rock formation. Follow the edge of this rock, maintaining a parallel trajectory. Continue straight until you reach a natural corner wall, then take a left turn.

Climb up the small hill directly in front of you. Once at the top, stick to the right-hand wall and proceed forward. Drop down the ledge carefully to minimize fall damage. Keep running straight until you reach the entrance of the Green Demon Cave. 

Enter the cave, immediately hug the left-hand wall, and exit through the opposite side. Once outside, stay to the left and run between the solitary tree and the adjacent boulder. You will spot a large Moyai statue directly ahead. Walk behind this statue, look to your left, and utilize the wall-climbing mechanic to ascend the tall cliff corner. Kokushibo will be waiting at the top of this plateau.

<Callout type="info" title="Navigational Landmarks">
There are two distinct Moyai statues in the wilderness. The correct statue for this guide is the one located further away from the Green Demon Cave exit, positioned directly below the final climbing cliff.
</Callout>

This combination creates a highly resilient build capable of outlasting opponents in extended PvP encounters and high-tier PvE raids.
