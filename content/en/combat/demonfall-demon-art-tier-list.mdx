<Section icon={Swords} title="SS-Tier & S-Tier Demon Arts Analyzed">

The top of the **demonfall demon art tier list** features arts that possess unblockable moves, hyper-armor properties, or unmatched resource regeneration. 

### Swamp (SS-Tier)
Swamp stands as the strongest Blood Demon Art in the game. Its defining feature is the unreactable grab, which acts as a premier combo starter. While technically blockable on paper, its startup frames are too fast for human reaction times. Additionally, Swamp features a 15-second cooldown iframe and an unblockable extender dealing 20 to 25 damage on a brief 20-second cooldown. Its only weak move costs 70 stamina, which top players simply avoid using.

### Hantengu (SS-Tier)
Hantengu is a powerhouse of stun lock and hyper-armor. It features a broken one-shot full combo initiated by a massive ragdoll stun that leaves opponents helpless for nearly seven seconds. This setup allows you to transition into a tornado combo starter. With a 20-second backend cooldown and five highly effective moves, Hantengu dominates ranked matchmaking.

### Akaza (S-Tier)
Akaza is the most creative BDA in the game. Activating Compass Needle gives you free stamina and enhanced health regeneration, allowing you to play passively when needed. It excels in creative combo chains, such as combining flying planets into M2s, transitioning into air bullets, and finishing with flurry kicks. Its only drawback is slightly lower raw damage compared to SS-tier options.

| Blood Demon Art | Best Move | Cooldown | Stamina Cost | Hyper-Armor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Swamp** | Unreactable Grab | 15s | Low | Yes |
| **Hantengu** | Ragdoll Tornado | 20s | Medium | Yes |
| **Akaza** | Compass Needle | Active | None (Regens) | No |
| **Dark Thunder** | RA Grab Loop | Short | Medium | Yes |
| **Nezuko** | Teleport Kick | Medium | 100 | No |

<Section icon={Shield} title="A-Tier & B-Tier Demon Arts">

These Blood Demon Arts are highly viable but require superior mechanical skill, precise stamina management, or psychological mind games to match the effectiveness of S-tier options.

### String (A-Tier)
String has maintained a stable position in the competitive meta. It easily achieves a 180-damage standing combo if you land your setup moves. However, playing String requires you to read your opponent's movement to land pulls, making it a high-risk, high-reward option.

### Sickles (A-Tier)
Sickles can nearly one-shot opponents if you land a full combo. It features excellent defensive options and a highly effective eye iframe. However, its high stamina costs prevent it from reaching S-tier. If you mismanage your resources, you will find yourself defenseless.

### Arrow (B-Tier)
Arrow functions as a specialized zoning tool. Its punishment move serves as an unblockable combo starter, and its fourth move provides excellent mobility. Previously, Arrow was top-tier due to fall damage scaling, but subsequent nerfs have reduced its overall damage output.

| Blood Demon Art | Combo Potential | Risk Level | Recommended Clan |

| **String** | High (180 Damage) | Medium | Iguro |
| **Sickles** | Extreme (Near One-Shot) | High | Kanroji |
| **Arrow** | Moderate | Low | Tamari |

<Callout type="info" title="Zoning Tip">
When playing Arrow, focus on utilizing your unblockable punishment starter to interrupt aggressive rushdown players. Do not attempt to trade blows directly; instead, play a hit-and-run style.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="C-Tier & D-Tier Demon Arts">

The lower-tier Blood Demon Arts suffer from critical design flaws, such as excessive stamina costs, lack of hyper-armor, or highly predictable attack animations.

### Shadow (C-Tier)
Shadow has struggled to find a place in the meta for multiple seasons. Its only saving grace is a reliable iframe. Beyond that, its blade attacks lack hyper-armor and are easily interrupted. Additionally, its primary setups can be escaped using standard defensive maneuvers.

### Ice (D-Tier)
Despite its popularity among some players, Ice struggles in competitive PvP. Almost every offensive move costs an absurd 70 stamina. Furthermore, Ice Slide is now blockable, removing its effectiveness as a reliable initiator. Its only valuable tool is its counter, meaning it must be paired with Soryu to achieve any success.

### Daki (D-Tier)
Daki is currently the weakest BDA in Demonfall. Designed as an aggressive rushdown art, its extreme stamina costs (40+ per move) force players into a slow, defensive playstyle. Its M1 animations are slow and easy to perfect-block even on high ping, and opponents can easily jump over its M2 attacks.

| Blood Demon Art | Stamina Per Move | Blockable Moves | Primary Defect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shadow** | Medium | Yes | No hyper-armor on blade moves |
| **Ice** | 70 (Extremely High) | Yes | Ice Slide is easily blocked |
| **Daki** | 40+ (Very High) | Yes | Low speed and easily parried M1s |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Low Tiers">
Avoid using Ice or Daki in ranked PvP. The stamina penalties alone will cause you to lose matches against players of equal skill levels using more efficient arts.
</Callout>
