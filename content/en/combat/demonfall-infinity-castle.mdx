<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Strategy: Akaza the Upper Moon Three">

The first major hurdle in the **demonfall infinity castle** is Akaza. Upon entering his chamber, he will immediately target Giyu Tomioka. Your primary objective is to deal as much damage as possible while Giyu acts as your primary tank.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Wait for Akaza to target Giyu. When Akaza commits to an M2 (heavy attack) on the NPC, use your **Lunge** to strike him and follow up with a 3-4 hit M1 combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Peeling for Giyu">
    If Giyu gets trapped in a combo, use a **Shoulder Bash** or a high-knockback breathing move to stagger Akaza. This resets the AI and keeps your distraction alive longer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Move Identification">
    Akaza uses two punch variants. The **Dash Punch** must be dodged sideways. The **Teleport Punch** is blockable; keep your guard up if he disappears from your screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Solo Transition">
    Once Giyu is defeated, you must bait Akaza's barrage. Block his initial hits, wait for the barrage animation to finish, then counter with a Shoulder Bash and a full combo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Akaza Move | Counter Action | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Barrage** | Block then Shoulder Bash | ★★★★☆ |
| **Dash Punch** | Side-dash / Dodge | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Teleport Kick** | Hold Block | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Destructive Death** | Distance / Evasive Move | ★★★★★ |

<Callout type="success" title="Healing Opportunity">
If you are a Hybrid, wait until Giyu is downed. You can pick him up and carry him into the next room or absorb him immediately to enter the Doma fight with full health.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Strategy: Doma and the Ice Clones">

Doma presents a different challenge in the **demonfall infinity castle**. His fight focuses on area denial and crowd management. You will be assisted by Shinobu Kocho, who is vital for managing Doma's clones.

When Doma spawns his ice clones, do not waste your ultimate moves on them. Let Shinobu engage the clones while you maintain focus on the main body. Doma's ice pillars and slides are his most dangerous moves, but they have a distinct wind-up animation that can be cancelled.

**Tactical Checklist for Doma:**
- **Double Jump**: Always be ready to double jump when you see the ground frost over to avoid the Ice Vines.
- **Lunge Spam**: Use Lunge whenever Doma starts the animation for his Ice Pillar to cancel the move.
- **Evasive Usage**: Save your breathing style's evasive move specifically for his grab or if you get caught in multiple ice slides.

| Doma Ability | Visual Cue | Best Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ice Pillars** | Raising arms upward | **Lunge** to cancel |
| **Ice Vines** | Ground turns white | **Double Jump** |
| **Ice Clones** | Backwards leap | Let Shinobu distract |
| **Ice Slide** | Leaning forward | Block or Side-dash |

<Callout type="info" title="Loot Note">
Doma has a rare chance to drop **Doma's Cape**. This accessory is highly sought after for its aesthetic value and slight defensive buffs in the 2026 update.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Final Encounter: Kokushibo and the Six Eyes">

The final boss of the **demonfall infinity castle** is Kokushibo, the Upper Moon One. This fight is a test of patience. You will have Sanemi Shinazugawa assisting you, but he often dies quickly due to Kokushibo's massive AOE damage.

**Phase 1: Support Strategy**
While Sanemi is alive, focus on "chip damage." Do not over-commit to long combos, as Kokushibo's Moon Breathing has wide arcs that can hit you even if you are behind him. Stay at a mid-range distance and use your projectile-based breathing moves.

**Phase 2: Solo Survival**
After Sanemi falls, the fight becomes a game of baiting. 
- **Long Range**: Keep your block up constantly. His crescent moon blades can travel across the entire arena.
- **Close Range**: Circle around him to bait his barrage move. Once he misses the barrage, you have a 2-second window to land a combo.

| Reward | Drop Chance | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kokushibo's Blood** | Rare | Grants **Six Eyes** (Demons/Hybrids) |
| **Dark Ore** | Common | Crafting material |
| **Prestige XP** | Guaranteed | Significant progression boost |

<Callout type="warning" title="One-Shot Mechanic">
Kokushibo's ultimate Moon Breathing form can one-shot most players if they are caught without stamina. Never let your stamina bar drop below 20% during this fight.
</Callout>
