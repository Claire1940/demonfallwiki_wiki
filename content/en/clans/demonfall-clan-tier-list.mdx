Kamado is widely considered the ultimate choice for players pursuing a Demon or Hybrid path. The sunlight immunity passive removes the threat of taking continuous damage in daytime environments, allowing you to farm freely. Additionally, the +20% experience boost significantly cuts down the grind to max level.

Tomioka is the premier choice for PvE and story progression. By starting with Lunge and Shoulder Bash automatically unlocked, you save valuable skill points that can be redirected into your breathing tree. It also grants the 11th form of Water Breathing, Dead Calm, which is a top-tier defensive tool.

For players focusing purely on damage output, Kanroji offers a flat +15% damage multiplier on all attacks, making it highly versatile for any breathing style or blood demon art.

Among the S-tier clans, Himejima stands out by offering a +30 HP boost. This extra health pool is incredibly forgiving for beginners who struggle with parry timings. Iguro focuses on mobility, granting free Dash Mastery and increasing walk speed (+2 for Slayers, +4 for Demons). Agatsuma features a unique Berserk passive: when downed, you enter a 45-second state with +50% damage and 20% health, allowing you to turn the tide of a battle or prevent an enemy from eating you in PvE.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Analyzing A and B Tier Clans">

A and B tier clans offer decent utility, but they are generally outclassed by the top-tier options. Many of these clans are highly specialized, meaning they only excel when paired with specific breathing styles or in niche scenarios.

Rengoku is a strong pick if you plan to run Flame Breathing. It provides a +20% flame damage buff, which stacks with the burn damage of Flame M1 attacks, dealing consistent damage over time. It also unlocks the 9th form of Flame Breathing. Ko is tailored for PvP, adding poison damage to your attacks to slowly drain your opponent's health during neutral play. 

Shinazugawa offers a +25% faster health regeneration rate, which is excellent for sustaining through long PvE fights, though it does increase your hunger drain rate. Tokito is another solid starter option, offering a +10% EXP boost and unlocking the 7th form of Mist Breathing, which is one of the easiest breathing styles to acquire early on.

In the B-tier, Hashibira acts as a weaker version of Shinazugawa, providing only a +10% health regeneration rate along with a cosmetic bore head accessory. Ubuyashiki provides a +10% EXP boost and starts you with two extra skill points, but its reduced vision range makes it difficult to navigate dark areas. Haganezuka resides in the C-tier and offers no combat utility, making it an automatic reroll.

| **Tokito** | A | +10% EXP, 7th Mist Form | Outclassed by Kamado |
| **Hashibira** | B | +10% Health Regen, Bore Head | Low regeneration value |
