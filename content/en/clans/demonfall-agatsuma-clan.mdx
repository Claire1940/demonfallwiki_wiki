<Section icon={Map} title="How to Learn Thunder Breathing">

To maximize the potential of the **demonfall agatsuma clan**, you must pair it with Thunder Breathing. This breathing style is taught by Kojima, the retired lightning hashira, who resides in a remote house within the Coast Forest. Before you can speak to him, you must prepare a substantial amount of resources to satisfy his training requirements.

First, travel to Okia Village and purchase 20 delicious soups from the local soup shop, which will cost you a total of 400 Yen. Additionally, you must accumulate 4,000 Yen to pay Kojima's final instruction tax. Once you have gathered these resources, make your way to Falls Bridge and take a detour to find his hidden cabin.

| Quest Step | Objective Details | Requirements | Key Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Step 1** | Purchase food from Okia Village | 20 Delicious Soups (400 Yen) | Quest items secured |
| **Step 2** | Deliver soups to Kojima | Visit Coast Forest cabin | Quest progression |
| **Step 3** | Deliver confidential message | Travel to Slayer Headquarters | Find Maron NPC |
| **Step 4** | Pay the final training tax | Return to Kojima with cash | **Thunder Breathing** unlocked |

After completing Kojima's errands and paying the fee, you will unlock the basic forms of Thunder Breathing. To make this style viable for continuous combat, you must also obtain infinite breathing. Travel to the Stone Person NPC and purchase a training rock for 300 Yen. Hold this rock continuously for approximately 10 minutes until your stamina no longer drains, granting you infinite breathing capacity.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Collect all 20 soups and the 4,000 Yen before you ever visit Kojima. This saves you from having to run back and forth across the map, which can easily take up to 30 minutes of real-world time.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Forging the Thunder Nichirin Blade">

A true Agatsuma build is incomplete without the custom Thunder Nichirin blade. This weapon provides a significant boost to your base slashing damage and infuses all your basic attacks with electrical elements. Crafting this blade requires completing the Slayer Exam and gathering several rare materials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pass the Slayer Exam">
    Travel to the Glina Forest and speak to the examiner. You must survive and defeat 30 demons before the timer reaches zero. Watch out for the blue demon, as it is highly aggressive. Passing the exam awards you the standard Slayer uniform and a base Nichirin blade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mine Sun Ore">
    Purchase a pickaxe for 500 Yen from any major village merchant. Travel to the mountain cliffs near Falls Bridge and mine the glowing ore veins to collect at least 20 pieces of Sun Ore.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Crystal Essence">
    Obtain a Crystal Essence by defeating cavern bosses or trading with other players. This is a rare drop, so trading is often the fastest method if you have spare trinkets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm 10,000 Yen">
    Search the map for Perfect Crystals, which sell to merchants for 6,000 Yen each. Combine this with general trinket farming to quickly accumulate the required 10,000 Yen forging fee.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Crafting Ingredient | Required Quantity | Primary Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slayer Nichirin** | 1 | Base reward for passing the Slayer Exam |
| **Sun Ore** | 20 | Mined from mountain cliffs |
| **Crystal Essence** | 1 | Cavern boss drop or player trading |
| **Yen** | 10,000 | Selling Perfect Crystals and trinkets |

Once you have all the ingredients, return to the blacksmith at the Slayer Headquarters to forge your yellow-tinged Thunder Nichirin.

<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Battle Tactics & Progression Route">

To test the limits of your **demonfall agatsuma clan** build, you must progress through the main story campaign. The campaign features several iconic bosses that require specific tactical approaches to defeat.

### Lower Moon 6 (Cavern Boss)
This massive boss is slow but hits incredibly hard. His fire breath can deal continuous damage if you get trapped. To defeat him easily, bait out his fire breath, dash behind him using Thunderclap and Flash, and execute your full combo. When he enters his rage phase, back away to avoid his explosive AOE slam.

### Lower Moon 5 (Rui)
Rui uses thread-based attacks that can trap you from a distance. Maintain a defensive posture and block his initial thread strikes. Once his attack sequence ends, use your gap closers to get close and unleash your high-damage forms.

### Lower Moon 2
This is one of the most challenging fights because of his stamina-draining aura. He will constantly deplete your stamina, preventing you from running or executing skills. If you get knocked down, let **Sleepy Awake** activate to instantly recover with a 50% damage boost, then quickly finish him off before the buff expires.

| Boss Name | Threat Level | Key Attack to Avoid | Winning Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lower Moon 6** | Low | Continuous Fire Breath | Dash behind during fire breath; punish slow turn speed |
| **Rui (Lower Moon 5)** | Medium | Thread Trap & Ranged Slashes | Block thread strikes; close distance with Thunderclap |
| **Lower Moon 2** | High | Stamina Drain Aura | Leverage **Sleepy Awake** knockdown buff to burst down |
| **Kaigaku** | Very High | Dark Thunder Breathing | Use shoulder bash to break guard; dodge AOE strikes |

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Tactics">
During the Kaigaku fight, do not attempt to trade blows directly. His Dark Thunder Breathing has superior reach and can easily interrupt your skills. Instead, wait for him to finish his skill animations before counter-attacking.
</Callout>
