<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking Flame Breathing: Step-by-Step">

Once you are part of the **demonfall rengoku clan**, your next objective is to master Flame Breathing. Unlike some styles that are taught in villages, Flame Breathing requires you to venture to the dangerous heights of **White Peak**. You will need to prove your worth to the Rengoku NPC by completing two difficult collection tasks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the NPC">
    Travel to White Peak. The Rengoku NPC is located high up on the mountain, often standing near the edge overlooking the valley.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Demon Horns">
    You must collect **20 Demon Horns**. These drop from basic demons found throughout the map, especially at night or in caves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Demon Boss">
    The NPC will ask for a **Demon Collar**. You must find and kill the specific demon boss in the cave at White Peak to obtain this item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Training">
    Return to the NPC with the items. He will grant you the Flame Breathing style, unlocking the first five forms in your skill tree.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazard">
White Peak is extremely cold and filled with high-level demons. Ensure you have enough soup for health regeneration before attempting the climb.
</Callout>

| **Demon Collar** | 1 | White Peak Cave Boss |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Flame Breathing Moveset and Ninth Form">

The true power of the **demonfall rengoku clan** lies in its expanded moveset. While standard Flame Breathers stop at the fifth form, Rengoku clan members can unlock the Ninth Form: Rengoku. This move involves a high-speed dash that creates a massive fire dragon, dealing multi-hit damage to anyone caught in its path.

| Form Number | Name | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **First Form** | Unknowing Fire | A high-speed charge followed by a single slash. |
| **Second Form** | Rising Scorching Sun | An upward arched slash perfect for launching enemies. |
| **Third Form** | Flame Bend | A circular slash that deflects incoming projectiles. |
| **Fourth Form** | Blooming Flame Undulation | A defensive counter-move that parries attacks. |
| **Fifth Form** | Flame Tiger | A series of slashes that take the form of a tiger. |
| **Ninth Form** | **Rengoku** | **Clan Exclusive**: A devastating dash with massive AOE. |

<Callout type="success" title="Infinite Breathing">
To maximize these moves, go to Kamakari Village and buy a **Boulder** for 300 Yen. Use it to train your breathing until your stamina bar no longer drains while holding your breath. This allows you to spam forms without pause.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Forging the Flame Nichirin">

To complete your transformation into the Flame Hashira, you must forge a custom **Flame Nichirin**. This requires traveling to the Slayer Headquarters and speaking with the blacksmith. Note that the default color is random, so you may need to reroll to get the iconic red blade.

| Item | Cost | Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slayer Nichirin** | Free | Complete Final Selection |
| **Flame Nichirin** | 10,000 Yen | 1 Crystal Essence + 20 Sun Ore |
| **Color Reroll** | 3,000 Yen | Random chance for Red/Orange |
| **Rengoku Hilt** | Special | Requires Rengoku Clan membership |

<Callout type="tip" title="Blade Color">
If you have Robux to spare, rerolling the blade color costs **50 Robux**. Red is the most accurate for a Rengoku build, but Orange also complements the flame effects well.
</Callout>

</Section>
