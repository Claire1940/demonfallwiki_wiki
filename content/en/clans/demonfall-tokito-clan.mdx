<Section icon={Shield} title="Infinite Breathing & Stamina Management">

A major hurdle for any Mist user is stamina. Mist Breathing forms consume a significant amount of breath meter. To overcome this, players must undergo "Infinite Breathing" training. This involves a specific training method in Kamakura Village that permanently upgrades your breathing capacity.

**The Boulder Training Method:**
1. Travel to **Kamakura Village**.
2. Purchase the **Boulder** item for 300 Yen from the local vendor.
3. Equip the Boulder and hold it. Your stamina will begin to drain rapidly.
4. Continue holding the Boulder as your health depletes. You must survive this process for approximately 10 minutes.
5. Once your stamina stops decreasing while holding the rock, you have achieved Infinite Breathing.

| Training Step | Action | Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Purchase** | Buy Boulder (300 Yen) | Obtain training tool |
| **Execution** | Hold Boulder until drain stops | Increase Breath capacity |
| **Result** | Infinite Breathing | No breath meter depletion |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Advice">
Infinite Breathing is mandatory for high-level play. Without it, you will find yourself unable to use moves during long boss fights like those against Kokushibo or Akaza.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Forging the Mist Nichirin Blade">

To complete the Muichiro Tokito look and maximize your damage, you need the **Mist Nichirin Blade**. This weapon is forged at the Demon Slayer Headquarters and requires specific rare materials.

**Required Materials for Mist Nichirin:**
- **20 Sun Ore**: Mined from caves or found in the mountainous regions.
- **1 Crystal Essence**: A rare drop or tradeable item.
- **10,000 Yen**: The forging fee for the specialized blade.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Ores">
    Purchase a pickaxe for 500 Yen and head to the mountains. Focus on Sun Ore deposits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Crystal Essence">
    Crystal Essence is rare. You can obtain it by defeating bosses or trading with other players in the hub.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Final Forging">
    Speak to the smith at Headquarters. Ensure you have exactly 10,000 Yen. Choose the Mist Nichirin option.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once forged, the blade typically appears with a white or light blue tint, perfectly matching the aesthetic of the Mist Hashira. This blade provides a hidden damage buff to all Mist Breathing forms.

| Material | Quantity | Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sun Ore** | 20 | Mining (Pickaxe required) |
| **Crystal Essence** | 1 | Boss Drop / Player Trade |
| **Yen** | 10,000 | Farming Bosses / Quests |

<Callout type="warning" title="Yen Farming">
Farming 10,000 Yen can be tedious. The fastest way is to join "Upper Moon 6" raids in the Entertainment District. Even if you are lower level, staying near the fight will grant you massive XP and Yen rewards.
</Callout>
